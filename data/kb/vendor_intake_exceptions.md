# Vendor Intake Exceptions

Northstream Data Systems LLC is a prospective vendor currently under procurement and security review. Agreements with Northstream must not be auto-approved, even when the commercial terms look standard.

For Northstream Data Systems LLC, legal ops must confirm completion of vendor onboarding, security review, and procurement sign-off before signature.

If a contract otherwise appears compliant but the counterparty is subject to an intake exception, the case should be routed to human review with the counterparty exception cited as a reason.
