# Contract Review Playbook

Approved governing law positions are Delaware, New York, or England and Wales. Any other governing law should be routed for human review unless legal has pre-approved an exception.

Our preferred limitation of liability is fees paid under the agreement during the prior 12 months. Unlimited liability is not acceptable for standard vendor agreements and must always be escalated.

Auto-renewal clauses longer than 12 months require legal review. If the renewal period is unclear, the case should be routed for manual review.

Termination for convenience should be available with no more than 30 days' notice. Longer notice periods require review.

If the agreement involves customer or employee personal data, the document should reference an executed DPA or equivalent privacy addendum. Missing privacy language requires review.
